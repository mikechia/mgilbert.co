About

Joanna has been practicing yoga herself for over five years. Although her love of yoga began in a hot yoga studio, the main style she has focused on has been Vinyasa Flow. In Summer 2015 Joanna trained under the FLY Vinyasa school in Spain. FLY Vinyasa is characterised by its focused, creative and intelligent flow style, with an emphasis on alignment, form and safety.
